
# Synaptiq™ — The Hidden Ground Truth Hiring Intelligence Engine

### Reimagining Talent Discovery Beyond Traditional ATS Systems

Synaptiq is a next-generation talent intelligence platform engineered to uncover the *Hidden Ground Truth* behind candidate excellence. Built for the India.Runs Data & AI Challenge, the platform transcends conventional keyword matching by replicating the analytical rigor of elite technical recruiters through a sophisticated multi-layer ranking architecture.

Rather than rewarding superficial resume keywords, Synaptiq identifies candidates who demonstrate genuine evidence of building production-grade Search, Retrieval, Ranking, Matching, and Recommendation systems—the capabilities that truly drive modern AI products.

---

# Vision

Modern hiring platforms generate overwhelming volumes of "potentially relevant" candidates by relying on simplistic keyword correlations.

This creates a fundamental signal-to-noise problem:

* Generic profiles frequently outrank deeply technical specialists.
* Architectural expertise becomes diluted among buzzword-heavy resumes.
* Recruiters spend valuable time reviewing candidates who appear qualified but lack real-world implementation depth.

Synaptiq was designed to solve this problem.

The platform evaluates candidates through an intent-first framework that prioritizes demonstrated engineering capability, architectural sophistication, evaluation rigor, and product-centric experience.

The result is a ranking engine that surfaces exceptional talent hidden beneath conventional ATS visibility.

# Core Innovation

## Tiered Ground Truth Classification Framework

At the heart of Synaptiq lies a proprietary multi-tier relevance architecture.

Instead of assigning arbitrary linear scores, candidates are classified into explicit competency tiers that mirror the reasoning process of world-class technical recruiters.

### Tier Hierarchy

**Tier 5 — Production Retrieval Architects**

* Designed and deployed Retrieval, Ranking, Recommendation, or Matching systems at scale.
* Demonstrates expertise in evaluation frameworks such as NDCG, MAP, MRR, and A/B experimentation.
* Shows evidence of measurable business or product impact.

**Tier 4 — Advanced Applied AI Engineers**

* Built complex AI products and pipelines.
* Strong system design capability with substantial implementation depth.

**Tier 3 — Experienced Practitioners**

* Proven engineering foundation with meaningful project ownership.

**Tier 2 — Emerging Contributors**

* Demonstrates technical potential but lacks architectural depth.

**Tier 1 — Tool-Level Users**

* Familiarity with AI tooling without evidence of substantial engineering implementation.

**Tier 0 — Non-Relevant Profiles**

* Insufficient alignment with target intent.

This architecture ensures that genuine specialists consistently outrank keyword-optimized generalists.

# Distinguishing Capabilities

## Behavioral Twin Separation Engine

When candidates exhibit comparable technical competence, Synaptiq introduces a sophisticated behavioral differentiation layer.

The proprietary **Availability Index** leverages exponential decay modeling to prioritize candidates demonstrating stronger recent engagement and responsiveness.

This enables recruiters to identify not only the most qualified candidates, but also the most actionable opportunities.

---

## Honeypot Intelligence Layer

Traditional ATS platforms struggle to detect profile inflation.

Synaptiq actively identifies suspicious candidate signals through its **Honeypot Detection Engine**, including:

* Unrealistic career progression patterns
* Implausible title acceleration
* Skill inflation anomalies
* Experience inconsistencies
* Signal-to-credential mismatches

Questionable profiles are automatically isolated and excluded from high-confidence recommendations.

---

## Deterministic Explainability Framework

Every ranking decision is fully transparent.

Rather than generating speculative AI explanations, Synaptiq produces deterministic evidence-backed reasoning directly derived from candidate signals.

Recruiters can instantly understand:

* Why a candidate received a specific tier assignment
* Which competencies influenced ranking
* What architectural evidence was detected
* How evaluation expertise contributed to placement

This creates complete auditability while eliminating hallucinated justifications.

# Performance Engineering

Synaptiq employs a two-stage ranking architecture optimized for large-scale candidate evaluation.

### Stage 1 — SQL Proxy Retrieval

A highly optimized retrieval layer rapidly identifies the most relevant candidate subset from a large-scale talent repository.

### Stage 2 — Deep Intent Evaluation

The shortlisted candidate pool undergoes comprehensive tier classification, behavioral analysis, and explainability generation.

This architecture delivers an exceptionally responsive recruiter experience while preserving evaluation depth and ranking precision.
